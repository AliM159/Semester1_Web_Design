Websites about Protected Areas Issues

Protected areas, also known as conservation areas, are vital to the health of our planet. They are designated regions where human activity is restricted to conserve biodiversity, natural resources, and cultural heritage. These areas play a crucial role in safeguarding endangered species, preserving ecosystems, providing clean water and air, and mitigating the effects of climate change. In today's world, where biodiversity is declining at an alarming rate and environmental challenges are mounting, the establishment and effective management of protected areas are more important than ever.
