Websites about Protected Areas Issues

Protected areas are essential for conserving biodiversity, protecting ecosystems, and promoting sustainable development. According to the International Union for Conservation of Nature (IUCN), protected areas cover around 15% of the Earth's land surface. However, more needs to be done to protect these vital areas.
