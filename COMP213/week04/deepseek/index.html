Websites about Protected Areas Issues

Protected areas, also known as conservation areas, are regions designated for the protection of biodiversity and natural resources. These areas play a crucial role in preserving ecosystems, mitigating climate change, and supporting local communities. In today's world, where environmental degradation is a pressing issue, protected areas are more important than ever.
