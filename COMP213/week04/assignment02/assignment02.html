Websites About Protected Areas Issues



Human activity, particularly in food production and forestry, has significantly contributed to land degradation, especially in drylands, threatening biodiversity and ecosystem services; however, effectively managed and well-governed protected areas, encompassing forests, marine ecosystems, and World Heritage sites, can help combat these challenges by preserving vital resources, supporting sustainable livelihoods, and promoting long-term environmental and socio-economic stability.
