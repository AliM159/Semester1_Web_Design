Websites about Protected Areas Issues

Protected areas, also known as conservation areas, are essential in preserving biodiversity, protecting endangered species, and promoting sustainable ecosystems. They are designated spaces where human activity is managed and restricted to ensure environmental protection. With threats like climate change, deforestation, and habitat loss, the establishment of protected areas is crucial for maintaining the balance of nature and safeguarding the future of wildlife.
